About

Ekramul Haque is an environmental researcher and journalist based in Khulna, Bangladesh. He is currently pursuing a Master of Science in Forestry at Khulna University, where his academic work focuses on land use and land cover dynamics, environmental change, and conservation policy.

His ongoing graduate research investigates the spatiotemporal dynamics of land use and land cover in the Sundarbans Ecologically Critical Area (2000–2025), using remote sensing and GIS techniques including Google Earth Engine and ArcGIS Pro.

Beyond academia, Ekramul is an active environmental journalist who translates complex scientific findings into accessible narratives that inform public discourse on climate change, biodiversity loss, and environmental governance.
